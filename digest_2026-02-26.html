  • This surfaces a real Experience Design opportunity—the onboarding, guidance, and content strategy for a 'learning' AI product looks nothing like a 'shortcut' AI product. We should be stress-testing their UX against both personas.
  • Here's the uncomfortable question: if your AI product genuinely works better for the 'avoid learning' crowd, does your brand want to say that out loud? Because honesty about who benefits most could actually be more defensible than trying to be everything to everyone.
